About Me

I am currently a MS student in Software Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am also one of the student execs for Women in ECE and ECE Graduate Organization at the Silicon Valley campus, so if you are interested in collaborating on a networking event, please reach out to me at clarindaho (at) cmu (dot) edu.

I completed my undergraduate at Case Western Reserve University, where I majored in Computer Science and minored in Chinese. During the course of my college career, I interned at Qualcomm, Amazon Web Services and Roche Sequencing Solutions as a software engineer.

During my free time, I love to read books, sing, play piano, travel, try out different cuisines throughout the world, and play tennis and badminton.
